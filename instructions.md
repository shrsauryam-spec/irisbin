## Assembly Instructions
First of all, print all the 3d parts separately (Remember to print **SIX** copies of "Iris_Flaps.stl". After printing is complete, take all the Iris Parts and then connect the Iris Flaps first to the Iris Connectors and then connect them to the Iris Bottom and Iris Gear, respectively. After that, insert the Iris into the "Mid Case". The Iris Bottom part should be attached to the hole of the Case properly. After that is complete, take the servo motor and put it inside the case so that the head of it stays outside the smaller hole in the case. Connect the servo head (first, connect the male-female jumper wires to the motor) to the small gear. Place it in such a way that the small gear and Iris Gear can easily touch each other. After that is complete, take the wires of the servo and take it out of the hole in the upper part of the case. And then, using the magnets specified in the README, connect the Case Base to the Mid Case. Next, place the Arduino Nano with the Enclosure in the MidCase Upper Part, and place the battery in the same part. Then, put the Sensor Enclosure with the Sensor on the front holes of the top part in the Mid Case. Connect the wires as shown in the wiring diagram. After all of this is complete, put the Case_Top2 and the Mid Case Upper Part together using magnets.
